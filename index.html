ABOUT DEPARTMENT


The Department of Chemistry is one of the first Departments to function after the Walchand College of Engineering, Sangli was founded in 1959. 

The Department began with one Professor and one lecturer as faculty members, to conduct classes for the undergraduate engineering programme of the Institute.
